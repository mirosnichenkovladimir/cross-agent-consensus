# Policy

Review Scope:

- Objective: verify the use-case document is concise, minimal, and behavior-complete for the current protocol/profile.
- In scope: missing protocol behavior, incorrect expected outcomes, unnecessary duplication, lifecycle/consensus/escalation/validation/scope mismatches.
- Out of scope: exhaustive examples, schema formalization, runner/plugin behavior, wording polish.

Review mode: `fresh_review`.

Consensus predicate:

- No unresolved in-scope blocking findings.
- Non-blocking suggestions may be listed separately.
